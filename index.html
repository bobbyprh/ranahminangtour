Our Story, Our Passion

Born from a deep-rooted love for our homeland, Ranah Minang Tour was founded with a singular purpose: to share the raw beauty, breathtaking landscapes, and profound cultural heritage of West Sumatra with the world. We don't just guide you to a destination; we invite you to immerse yourself in the authentic stories and traditions of our people.

Every tour is a journey of discovery, meticulously crafted to ensure you feel the warmth of Minangkabau hospitality and experience a land where every mountain, lake, and village holds a piece of history. We are more than a tour company; we are storytellers, and we can't wait to share our story with you.

Popular Destinations

Lake Maninjau

A breathtaking caldera lake surrounded by lush green hills, offering a serene escape and a challenge with its famous 44 hairpin turns.

Bukittinggi City

The vibrant heart of Minangkabau culture, a city rich with history, unique architecture, and the iconic Jam Gadang clock tower.

Harau Valley

A paradise for nature lovers, this majestic canyon is a series of towering granite cliffs and stunning waterfalls.

Sianok Canyon

A magnificent ravine located just outside of Bukittinggi, offering incredible panoramic views and a tranquil environment.

Padang City

Explore the bustling capital city, famous for its delicious Padang food and vibrant waterfront.

Mentawai Islands

A surfer's paradise, known for world-class waves, pristine beaches, and unique tribal culture.

Pagaruyung Palace

Visit the magnificent replica of the former royal palace of the Pagaruyung Kingdom, a symbol of Minangkabau culture.

Anai Valley Waterfall

A stunning waterfall surrounded by a lush forest and a historic railway track, making for a picturesque stop.
